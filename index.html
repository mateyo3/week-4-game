The Anbu Mask Game

Minato will give you a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Anbu masks below. By clicking on a mask you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Anbu mask is hidden from you until you click on it.

When the game restarts, the game will change the values of each Anbu mask.
